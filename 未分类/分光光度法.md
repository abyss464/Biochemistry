### 主要公式 (吸光度形式)

$$
A = \epsilon l c
$$

*   $A$ = 吸光度 (Absorbance)
*   $\epsilon$ = 摩尔吸光系数 (Molar absorptivity)
*   $l$ = 光程长度 (Path length)
*   $c$ = 溶液浓度 (Concentration)

---

### 与透光率的关系

$$
A = -\log_{10}(T) = \log_{10}\left(\frac{1}{T}\right)
$$

$$
T = \frac{I}{I_0}
$$

*   $T$ = 透光率 (Transmittance)
*   $I_0$ = 入射光强度 (Incident light intensity)
*   $I$ = 透射光强度 (Transmitted light intensity)

---

### 综合形式 (指数形式)

$$
\frac{I}{I_0} = 10^{-A} = 10^{-\epsilon l c}
$$
